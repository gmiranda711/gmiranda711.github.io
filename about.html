About Me

Ever since she was able to hold a pencil, Gaby knew she would immerse herself into the arts. By the time she was in elementary school, her teachers noticed her artistic talents and would have her help decorate their bulletin boards with her drawings. Born and raised in Miami, FL by a Cuban family of artists, her passion for the arts was welcomed and encouraged. During her time in high school, she began drawing more and concentrating on portraits. She would draw celebrities and rappers other teens admired like Bob Marley, Kid Cudi and Wiz Khalifa.

 

 She is now currently enrolled at Miami International University of Art & Design majoring in Visual Arts with a focus on painting. As of late, Gaby has been focusing on oil paintings exploring portraiture, landscapes, and nature in a realistic, surrealistic and impressionistic style. She enjoys spending time outdoors, exercising, doing yoga, reading, and of course painting. She is currently working on opening an online shop to officially share her artwork with the world.
